Who We Are

Second Robotics is a volunteer-driven organization that began in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. When in-person robotics competitions were cancelled, our passionate group of volunteers came together to recreate the FRC event experience online, complete with live commentators and high-production value streams.

Today, we continue to run tournaments for Second Robotics Competition (SRC), Second Tech Challenge (STC), Second VEX Competition (SVC), and Minecraft Robotics Competition (MRC). Our events range from full-scale FRC-style tournaments to single-player showdowns and community challenges, all designed to capture the excitement and competitive spirit of in-person robotics competitions in a virtual format.

As the official competitive organization of xRC, our dedicated volunteer team works tirelessly to make virtual robotics competition accessible, exciting, and authentic for teams around the world.

Second Robotics Competition (SRC)

SRC is our flagship league, featuring the most competitive and exciting virtual robotics tournaments. In the past, SRC has hosted district seasons, single-player tournaments, ladder seasons, and large-scale championship events that bring together the best teams from around the world.

Whether you prefer team-based alliance competitions or solo skill challenges, SRC offers diverse competitive formats to showcase your robotics prowess and strategic thinking.
